How to Prepare the Perfect Steak

Step 1: Selecting the Steak

Choose a high-quality cut of steak, it's already chosen, just follow the preparation instructions.

Step 2: Seasoning

Season the steak generously with salt and pepper on both sides. You can also add other seasonings like garlic powder or rosemary for extra flavor.

Step 3: Preparing the Grill or Pan

Preheat your grill or pan to high heat. Make sure it's well-oiled to prevent sticking.

Step 4: Cooking the Steak

Place the steak on the hot grill or pan. For medium-rare, cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side.

Step 5: Resting

Let the steak rest for a few minutes before slicing. This allows the juices to redistribute, ensuring a juicy steak.
